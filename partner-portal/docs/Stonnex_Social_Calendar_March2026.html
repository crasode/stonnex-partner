Content Strategy
Two weeks to establish Stonnex as the go-to roofing brand on the Lower Mainland.

Goals

  • Establish brand presence from day one
  • Build trust with homeowners before they need a roof
  • Position Stonnex as premium without being inaccessible
  • Generate DMs and inspection requests
  • Create an aesthetic grid worth following

2026 Instagram Approach

  • One post per day builds momentum fast
  • Alternate photo and design/text tiles for grid rhythm
  • Educational content earns saves and reach
  • Golden hour and aerial shots drive profile visits
  • Minimal captions on strongest visuals
  • Direct, human captions — no corporate speak

Tone & Voice

  • Confident but never arrogant
  • Educational, not sales-forward
  • Local — name the neighbourhoods
  • Craft-focused — the work speaks
  • Human — a real team doing real work
  • Premium but approachable
